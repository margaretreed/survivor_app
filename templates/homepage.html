Welcome to your go to app for analyzing votes in the game of Survivor. Who is voted out each episode? By whom? Who has the strongest alliance? Explore interactive visualizations to help you discover the answers to these questions for every episode of Survivor across all 41 seasons.

Analyze vote histories with the help of interactive arc diagrams. These interactive diagrams tell a story of who each castaway voted for each episode. Highlight a castaway vote name to see all the players that voted for them.

Track strongest alliances across a season with the help of a heatmap showing the castaways that voted with each other the most often.

Never feel like you're missing a backstory again with our season watch recommendations. With each season, see how many castaways have appeared in seasons prior and the seasons they appear.
